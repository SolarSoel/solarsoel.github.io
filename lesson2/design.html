Visual Hierarchy

Nintendo
nintendo.com

The first thing you see on opening Nintendo is a bright advertisement for a holiday special. Even before searching for games, Nintendo makes sure to let you know of the current deals going on so the consumer can be prepared to make a purchase.

Hick's Law

D&D Beyond
dndbeyond.com

There is so much information about the game Dungeons and Dragons that searching can be overwhelming! Here on D&D Beyond, a hamburger menu lists all sort of menus in a quick drop down, providing even more detailed lists to help the user find whatever it is they may need. This makes the process of finding stray information quick and hassle free- no countless scrolling and page clicks to do.

Fitt's Law

Discord
discord.com

Here we see the call to action, Download Discord, is easily visible and accessable. With only a brief description of what the app is about, the prominent white button loads in quickly and is visible without any scrolling necessary. Being so front and center, it is hard to miss.
